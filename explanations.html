In order to become a citizen of the United States, an immigrant has to pass a test. This can be as scary as any big college exam.

Our app adds a bit of convenience to the studying process, and since the test is verbal, we let the user memorize the sounds of the questions and correct answers.

This app includes a practice test with fun motivational pictures, and explanations for various concepts found in U.S. civics.
